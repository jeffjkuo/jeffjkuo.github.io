About Me

Hello! I am an Economics Ph.D. candidate at The George Washington University .

I hold a M.A. in Economics from Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs at Syracuse University, and a B.A. in International Business and Trade from National Chengchi University. Before leaving to the U.S. for graduate study, I was a research assistant at the Institute of Economics, Academia Sinica.

My primary research interests are Trade, International Economics, and Macroeconomics. Currently, I am also a member of Research Program of Forecasting and Student Association for Graduate Economists.
